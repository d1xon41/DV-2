How much of each state is protected?

The ACT leads with over 55% of its land under protection, while NSW protects just 10%. Western Australia contributes the most area by sheer size.

02 — QUANTITY

Where are the most protected areas?

Victoria leads in sheer number of protected areas, driven by its dense network of small reserves and conservation agreements.

Number of Protected Areas by State

Victoria has nearly 5,000 individual protected areas — more than any other state.

State Area vs Protected Area

03 — CLASSIFICATION

How are protected areas classified?

The IUCN classification system categorises protected areas from strict nature reserves (Ia) to sustainable use areas (VI). Category VI areas dominate Australia's reserve system.

Area by IUCN Protection Category

Category VI (sustainable use) accounts for the vast majority of Australia's protected area by hectares, reflecting the inclusion of large Indigenous Protected Areas.
